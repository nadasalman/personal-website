Module designed and taught from scratch (30 hours total). Topics covered:

- Human factors, ergonomics and user-centred design
- System safety, accident prevention and risk analysis
- Visual perception: human vision, colour, luminance and visual interface design
- Auditory, tactile and vestibular perception applied to human-machine systems
- Haptics, force feedback and touch perception
- Teleoperation, force feedback and master-slave architectures
- Medical robotics: surgical, rehabilitation and assistive robots
- EMG signal acquisition and processing for robotic system control
