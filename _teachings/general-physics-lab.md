Practical lab supervision (128 hours total over two academic years). Topics covered:

- **Mechanics** — projectile motion, solid friction, experimental data acquisition and analysis
- **Geometric optics** — thin lenses, image formation, focal length, laws of reflection and refraction
- Experimental analysis tools and data processing
- Scientific methodology, uncertainty estimation and technical report writing
